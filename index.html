"If I could say it in words there would be no reason to paint."
Edward Hopper

Creating art is not just a hobby, but also a way in which I believe goes much deeper than what you see on the surface. In other words, a portal for you to see the world as I see it. It does not define who you are and the way you think but rather, a mode of expressing a certain state of mind.

It is the most therapeutic and eye-catching way to express your personal emotions and connect you to your inner self. An artist by profession and passion, my mother has always incorporated art into my life. Now, I view painting as a form of assisting and guiding me to mould myself into the individual I have become and am still becoming.

You can find a few of my personal favourite pieces, that have been created by me over the past years, here. These works are now in the homes of my lovely family and friends. If you'd like me to create a custom piece for you, please visit the order page.
